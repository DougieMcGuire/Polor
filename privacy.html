Polor

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2025
Quick Summary: Polor respects your privacy. We only collect what's necessary to provide our anonymous Q&A service and social media integrations. We never sell your data or share it without your permission.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a Polor account, we collect:

  • Email address (for login and account recovery)
  • Username (public identifier)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

Social Media Connections

When you connect social media accounts, we store:

  • Access tokens (encrypted) to post on your behalf
  • Social media usernames (for display purposes)
  • Profile information from connected platforms (username, display name)
  • We do NOT access your private messages, followers, or other personal data

Messages and Questions

  • Anonymous questions sent to you
  • Your responses to questions
  • Timestamps and basic analytics (view counts, share counts)
  • IP addresses (for spam prevention and security)

Usage Information

  • Pages visited on Polor
  • Features used and time spent
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Analytics data to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

Core Service

  • Delivering anonymous questions to your account
  • Enabling you to respond and share answers
  • Connecting your responses to social media platforms
  • Providing analytics about your Q&As

Account Management

  • Creating and maintaining your account
  • Authenticating your login
  • Sending important account notifications
  • Providing customer support

Social Media Integration

  • Posting your Q&A responses to connected platforms
  • Formatting content appropriately for each platform
  • Managing cross-platform sharing preferences

Safety and Security

  • Preventing spam and abuse
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Protecting user safety and platform integrity

3. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

Public Information

  • Your username and display name are public
  • Your Q&A responses are public (when you choose to answer)
  • Profile pictures and basic profile information

Social Media Platforms

  • We share your Q&A content with connected social platforms when you choose to post
  • This includes content text, formatting, and associated metadata
  • Each platform has its own privacy policy governing this data

Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting providers (Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • Analytics services (for platform improvement)
  • Customer support tools
  • All providers are bound by strict data protection agreements

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect user safety and platform integrity.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: All social media tokens and sensitive data are encrypted
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use Google Firebase with enterprise-grade security
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access with two-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and updates
  • HTTPS: All data transmission is encrypted

5. Data Retention

Messages

  • Unanswered questions: Deleted after 7 days
  • Read but unanswered: Deleted after 7 days
  • Answered questions: Deleted after 30 days
  • Public Q&As: Retained while account is active

Account Data

  • Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted within 90 days of account deletion
  • Some anonymized analytics may be retained longer

6. Your Rights and Choices

Account Control

  • Update Information: Edit your profile and settings anytime
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and associated data
  • Export Data: Download your Q&As and account information

Privacy Settings

  • Anonymous Questions: Enable/disable receiving questions
  • Social Sharing: Control which platforms to share to
  • Public Profile: Control what information is visible

Communications

  • Email Notifications: Opt out of non-essential emails
  • Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional content
  • Account Security: Security notifications cannot be disabled

7. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login and core functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns (can be disabled)
  • Social Media: Platform widgets may set their own cookies
  • No Advertising: We don't use cookies for targeted advertising

8. Third-Party Services

Social Media Platforms

When you connect social accounts, you're also subject to their privacy policies:

  • Twitter/X: twitter.com/privacy
  • Instagram/Facebook: facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Snapchat: snap.com/privacy/privacy-policy

Infrastructure Providers

  • Google Firebase: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Analytics: policies.google.com/privacy

9. International Users

Polor is operated from the United States. If you're using our service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We comply with applicable international privacy laws including GDPR for EU users.

10. Children's Privacy

Polor is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do:

  • We'll notify you via email or in-app notification
  • We'll update the "Last updated" date
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Material changes will require explicit consent

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information:

  • Email: privacy@polor.org
  • Support: help@polor.org
  • Website: polor.org

We'll respond to privacy inquiries within 72 hours.
